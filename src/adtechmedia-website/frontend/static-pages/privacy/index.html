Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2017-01-23.

This privacy policy applies to the website at www.adtechmedia.io (the "Site").
The Site provides access to AdTechMedia (the "Platform") -- an advertising platform with micropayments capabilities for media content monetization. The Platform empowers publishers and content providers (the "Business Customers") to generate complementary revenue streams that are immune to ad blocking software. The Business Customers can easily and seamlessly integrate the Platform into their web sites or web apps, offering their web users (the "Content Consumers") options to either pay for content out of their pockets through micropayments or watch advertising to continue consuming for free.
The "Service" is defined as any service provided or enabled via the Platform.
We respect and protect the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your information and is part of our Terms of Use when you use the Site or the Service.

Your Consent

By using the Site or the Service, you consent to our Privacy Policy. If you don't agree, please don't use our Site or Service.

What information do we collect?

When you register for the Service, you'll be asked to provide your email address and optional password. Further, Business Customers will be required to provide certain information about themselves and their company, such as first name and last name, company name, employer identification number or tax identification number, bank account number, and billing address.
Content Consumers will be required to provide certain information such as first name, last name, and email address, as well as information about their payment gateway accounts, such as (but not limited to) Apple Pay, Android Pay, PayPal, Amazon Payments, Stripe (the "Payment Gateways"), each of which has its own legal terms. The Payment Gateways will generate time-limited identity tokens, which the Platform will retain for further use.
We may also collect non-personal information, including information that can't be used to personally identify you as an individual, such as:
  • anonymous usage data
  • referring/exit pages and URLs
  • platform types
Additionally, we may collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit the Site or the Service ("Log Data"). The Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our Site or Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages and other statistics. We may use your public IP address to detect your location, so that we know where our potential users are coming from. Also, the Site or the Service may not be available in all geographical areas.

How do we use your information?

Information and User Content we collect from you might be used:
  • To provide you with Services
  • To improve our Site and Services
  • For marketing purposes

Do we use cookies?

Yes. Cookies are small files that include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies let us recognize your browser and remember certain information about you in order to personalize your experience of our Site and Service.
We also use cookies to compile aggregate data about traffic to the Site or the Service, so that we can improve our Site or Service.
We may use both persistent and session cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your computer after you close your session and until you delete them; session cookies expire when you close your browser.

How long do we store your information?

We intend to store your information and User Content indefinitely.

What about links to other websites?

We may provide links to or compatibility with other websites. However, we're not responsible for the privacy practices employed by those websites or the information or content they contain.

How do we protect your information?

We use appropriate physical, electronic, and other procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect. However, please be aware that the Internet is an inherently unsafe environment, and that hackers are constantly working to defeat security measures.
Thus, we cannot guarantee that your information will not be accessed, disclosed, altered or destroyed, and you accept this risk.

How can you protect your information?

We urge you to take steps to keep your personal information safe by not sharing it or your password with others.

Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personal information for commercial or marketing purposes.
We will, if required by a valid court order, provide your personal information in a civil or criminal proceeding.

Not Intended for Children

Both the Site and the Service are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly or specifically collect information from or about children under the age of 13.

Changes to our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on this page. We may also, but are not required to, send you an email notice.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@adtechmedia.io.

About Us

AdTechMedia is an advertising platform with micropayments capabilities for media content monetization. We help publishers and content providers to generate complementary revenue streams that are immune to ad blocking software (e.g. native advertising or native micropayments). Our solutions are white labeled, data driven and realtime.
