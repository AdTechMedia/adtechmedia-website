Native Micropayments

Complementary revenue model that monetizes natively specific audiences (e.g. ad blocking users). Similar to online retailers, AdTechMedia empowers publishers and content providers with just-in-time payment solutions, offering payment choices to their end users without taking them away from the reading experience.

Benefits

  • Native seamless experience
  • Low barrier of entry
  • Frictionless charges
  • No questions asked refunds

White-Label Technology

Publishers and content providers are empowered to own and drive their customers' experience. Every step in our Business-to-Business solution is customizable and optimizable through business rules that can be turned on and off.

Benefits

  • Customizable look and feel
  • Reduced operational complexity
  • Compliant with security standards

Data-Driven Decisions

W. Edwards Deming says that "without data you are just another person with an opinion". AdTechMedia helps publishers and content providers to focus on what really works for them. We constantly evolve through data analytics centered solutions to reach realtime capabilities in everything we do.

Benefits

  • Define key success business metrics
  • Highly performing business rules
  • A/B test ideas quickly & take actions

About Us

AdTechMedia is an advertising platform with micropayments capabilities for media content monetization. We help publishers and content providers to generate complementary revenue streams that are immune to ad blocking software (e.g. native advertising or native micropayments). Our solutions are white labeled, data driven and realtime.
