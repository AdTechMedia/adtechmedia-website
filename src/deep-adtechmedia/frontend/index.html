challenges

According to a recent study ad blocking grew by 41% globally to over 200M active users in the last 12 months, costing publishers an estimated $22B during 2015. Another study concludes that at least 419M active users (22% of the world's 1.9B smartphone users) are blocking ads on the mobile web.

This is a manifestly unsavoury business practice. Adblockers often portray themselves as an answer to unsatisfactory digital advertising experiences. But Eyeo wasn't founded by concerned citizens. It was founded by a digital ad veteran and represents the most cynical, most money-grasping end of the old unreformed digital ad business.
Many people already receive our journalism for free online, with digital advertising paying only a portion of the cost. Without income via subscriptions or advertising, we are unable to deliver the journalism that people coming to our site expect from us.
The Newspaper Association of America (NAA) today (May 26, 2016) filed a Complaint and Request for Investigation with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) alleging that certain ad blocking technologies and related services violate Section 5 of the FTC Act as unfair and deceptive trade practices.

solutions

Native Micropayments

Complementary revenue model that monetizes natively specific audiences (e.g. ad blocking users). Similar to online retailers, AdTechMedia empowers publishers and content providers with just-in-time payment solutions, offering payment choices to their end users without taking them away from the reading experience.

Benefits

  • Native seamless experience
  • Low barrier of entry
  • Frictionless charges
  • No questions asked refunds

White-Label Technology

Publishers and content providers are empowered to own and drive their customers' experience. Every step in our Business-to-Business solution is customizable and optimizable through business rules that can be turned on and off.

Benefits

  • Customizable look and feel
  • Reduced operational complexity
  • Compliant with security standards

Data-Driven Decisions

W. Edwards Deming says that "without data you are just another person with an opinion". AdTechMedia helps publishers and content providers to focus on what really works for them. We constantly evolve through data analytics centered solutions to reach realtime capabilities in everything we do.

Benefits

  • Define key success business metrics
  • Highly performing business rules
  • A/B test ideas quickly & take actions
